Privacy Policy

How Ignite Development collects, uses, and protects the information you share with us.

Last updated: June 2026

Ignite Development ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through our website, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using our site or contacting us, you agree to the practices described below.

Information We Collect

We only collect information you choose to provide. When you submit our contact form, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, company name, industry, team size, and any details you include in your message.

Our website may also automatically receive basic technical information — such as your browser type, device, and IP address — through standard web requests and the third-party services we use.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry and schedule a consultation
  • Understand your business and recommend appropriate services
  • Communicate with you about our work together

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to anyone.

Third-Party Services

We rely on a small number of trusted third-party services to operate our site:

  • Formspree — processes and delivers our contact form submissions.
  • Google Fonts — used to display the site's typography.

These providers may process limited data on our behalf and maintain their own privacy policies governing that use.

Data Retention

We keep the information you submit only as long as needed to respond to your inquiry and maintain our business relationship. You may ask us to delete it at any time.

Your Choices & Rights

You can request to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you by emailing us at contact@ignitedevelopment.net. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

Children's Privacy

Our website and services are intended for businesses and are not directed to individuals under 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, reach out at contact@ignitedevelopment.net.
