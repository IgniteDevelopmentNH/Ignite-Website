How we work

From conversation
to live system.

Every Ignite engagement follows a clear five-step framework — transparent, practical, and focused on getting real, working tools into your hands as quickly as possible.

Step 01
Discover
Step 02

Step 03
Design
Step 04

01

Discover

We start by deeply understanding your business — how it operates, what your team does day-to-day, where the bottlenecks are, and what you'd love to have running automatically.

This isn't a surface-level questionnaire. We ask the right questions and actually listen — because the best AI implementations start with a clear picture of the business, not a list of tools.

Business walkthrough Team interviews Workflow mapping Goal setting
02

Assess

We analyze everything we learned and identify the specific opportunities where AI can deliver immediate, measurable value for your business — prioritized by impact, feasibility, and ROI.

You'll understand exactly what's possible, what it would take, and what you'd gain. No vague promises — just a clear-eyed assessment of where AI fits your operation.

Opportunity identification ROI prioritization Tool evaluation Feasibility review
03

Design

We build a customized implementation plan — specific tools, clear timeline, transparent pricing. Every deliverable is defined before any work begins, so there are no surprises.

The plan is ordered to deliver value as fast as possible, with quick wins early and larger infrastructure built over time.

Written proposal Itemized pricing Phased timeline Defined deliverables
04

Implement

We build and deploy everything directly in your real environment — your actual data, your actual workflows, your actual customers. Not a demo. Not a sandbox. Production systems from day one.

We stay in close communication throughout, with regular updates and checkpoints to make sure everything is on track and meeting expectations.

Live deployment Real data integration Regular updates Testing & QA
05

Optimize

After launch, we monitor performance, gather feedback, and refine based on real-world results. We also train your team so they're fully equipped to use — and eventually expand — what we've built.

We stay available as your business evolves. AI implementation isn't a one-time project — it's an ongoing capability, and we're here to help you grow it.

Performance monitoring Team training Documentation Ongoing support
Our goal isn't simply to introduce AI.
Our goal is to help your business use AI effectively, responsibly, and profitably.
