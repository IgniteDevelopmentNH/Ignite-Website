How Personal Trainers Can Give Every Client AI Coaching

Joshua Berry
Co-Founder

I hit the gym the other day and used AI to walk me through my entire workout based on my goals. Real-time guidance, exercise selection, form cues, intensity levels.

Honestly? It was one of my best workouts. But here's what struck me: AI caught something I've been doing wrong since I started lifting. For years I've been training to failure on every set. I thought that was the way to maximize growth. But the AI explained RIR. Reps in Reserve. Stopping a set with a couple reps left in the tank actually allows for better progressive overload and better recovery.

I adjusted mid-workout and my training immediately felt smarter. More sustainable.

That's when it hit me: what if personal trainers could give every client this kind of coaching, every single time they train?

What We Build

We create custom software portals for personal training businesses. Here's how it works:

Clients Input Their Goals

When they sign up, they tell the software their fitness goals, experience level, and what matters to them. The software takes that information and builds a personalized training plan.

AI Coaches Them Through Every Workout

They log in before training and see their personalized plan for the day. The AI coaches them through every exercise with form cues, rep ranges, rest periods, and real-time guidance. It's like having a trainer in their pocket.

Track Everything

The software tracks whatever matters to your business:

  • Macros and calories
  • Progress over time
  • Strength gains
  • Body composition changes
  • Workout adherence
  • Whatever else you want to measure

Completely Customizable

We build it around what your business needs. Some trainers want macro tracking. Some want video form analysis. Some want nutrition integration. Some want community features. Whatever adds value for your clients, we can build it.

You tell us what you want, and we build it specifically for your business.

Why This Matters

Right now, personal trainers can only coach the hours they're physically present. Your clients leave the gym and they're on their own. With this software, they get professional coaching every single time they train.

They see better results because they're being coached consistently. They understand why they're training a certain way. They stay longer and refer more people.

What this means for your business

For you, it means you can serve more clients without working more hours. You scale without burning out. You offer a service that your clients actually want and will pay for.

It's software built specifically for your business. Not a generic app. Not something that costs a fortune. Just custom tools that solve your specific problems and add real value for your clients.
